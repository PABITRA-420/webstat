Design

SARKAR Wins Digital Innovation Award 2026

Jan 18, 2026

We are thrilled to announce that SARKAR has been recognized with the prestigious Digital Innovation Award 2026. This accolade celebrates our commitment to pushing the boundaries of web interactivity and physics-based design.

Our recent project, "Zero Gravity," demonstrated how web interfaces can mimic physical laws, creating a sense of weightlessness and fluidity that users have never experienced before. By leveraging advanced WebGL techniques and custom physics engines, we've redefined what a website can feel like.

"This award is a testament to our team's relentless pursuit of the impossible," says the founder. "We believe the future of the web is not just about information, but about feeling."
